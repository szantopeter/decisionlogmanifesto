Manifesto for Creating Decision Logs




We are uncovering better ways of documenting decisions
by doing it and helping others do it.
Through this work we have come to value:

Researching options over repeating decesions of the past
 Business value over doing things for for the joy of doing it
 Cost-benefit analysis over over politics or personal preferences
 Summary table over lengthy essays
 Central decisions registry over storing decisions in code or email
 Re evaluating decisions over following old habits

That is, while there is value in the items on
the right, we value the items on the left more.
